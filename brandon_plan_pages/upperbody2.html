Day 3 (Friday): Upper Body - Back Focus

- It's all about negative. Good contraction then slow release to the stretched position - Lock the shoulders by open the chest

Routines

1. Pull Up (Vertical)

Pull up with wide grip is the best to make wide back.
If it is too hard, you can focus on negative. Start from contraction position using support and then slowly go down.

2. Dumbbell Press/Fly (Range)

Select one:
1. Dumbbell Press
2. Dumbbell Fly
Dumbbell fly needs less weight but great for stretch position. Keep the back locked to reduce shoulder injury.

3. Rows (Horizontal)

Select one:
1. Barbel rows (Smith or Free)
2. Dumbbell rows (incline or standing)
This can be changed to pull up or chin up to focus more on lats (wide back).

Triceps Extension

Hold one dumbbell above head and slowly go down behind your head. Feel the stretch at the bottom. Sitting will let you focus on triceps and standing will allow you to go heavy. Change to other triceps exercises if you feel pain in elbows.
